The Basic

What is co-working?

Co-working is collectively working in a flexible and enriching environment that also surrounds you with other individuals from different industries and companies.

Who is warehouse eight for?

Co-working at Warehouse Eight does not only cater to people who simply need a space to work. It is for people who see the value in co-creation and potential for growth with an integrated community.

What should i bring to work at warehouse eight?

For co-working spaces, you can bring everything you would normally bring to a coffee shop. For our private and semi-private workspaces feel free to break into them, hang some posters, stick some post-its and add touches of YOU.

How productive can i be at the space?

Our members are welcome to over-use our co-working space, lounge and pantry. We all know how environment can play a big part in your productivity mojo, so the variety of space we provide offers flexibility to move around to wherever you may feel like at the moment.

I'm Interested, What's Next?

Do i have to make an appointment to see the space?

Although we welcome walk-ins, hit us up at the contact page and let us know when you're coming by, we can pencil you in and personally take you around the space. Our team will find the right fit for your company and accommodate to any special inquiries you may have!

No time to drop by?

Email us at hello@warehouse-eight.com or message us on our Facebook page and we can draft a proposal based on your needs with our rates and photos of the spaces you are interested in.

Should i wait till the 1st of the month to join

You are able to join any time but the move out date (billing) would be on our cut off which is the 15th of every month or at the end of the month.

What is the shortest time i can occupy a space?

For co-working spaces: 1 day For meeting rooms: 1 hour For semi-private spaces: 1 month For private spaces: 1 month

Our Space

Can i leave stuff on the table at the common areas

The Warehouse has CCTV cameras and biometric access at the entrance. If you have any valuables you’d like to keep extra safe, we have lockers available for you to use.

Hunger Pangs

The most convenient choice would be our two food and drink concessionaires at our ground floor. One of them is Brown Paper Project specialising in quality artisan coffee and small baked goods. Our concessionaires are start-ups who use our space as a short-term pop up for their business. If you prefer to bring your own packed lunches, we have a fridge and microwave free for you to use.

Meeting Room Usage?

You can still book our meeting rooms even if you are not a member of Warehouse Eight. We charge hourly. They're perfect for strategic team planning and discussions!

Is there a difference with the rates if i bring in my own office furniture?

Our rates are fixed with or without using the office furniture we provide. Although if you prefer to mix and match with ours or totally use your own furniture, you're welcome to!
